The assessment process is broken. Furthering inequality.
Burdening those who need a relief. And boosting those who don't need. 
AssessingAgents is an agentic Assessor's office team. 
Here to independently appraise and exempt properties. 
Producing a justice seeking simulation, meant to meaningfully inform the real assessment. 

In other words, AssessingAgents simulates appraisal and exemption.
You decide when and were. 

AssessingAgents is a team of 5. 
-Chief assessor: Orchestrator who oversees the team. Talks to the client. 
-Appraisal data engineer: collects data.
-Appraisal learning engineer: appraises properties. 
-Exemptions engineer: exempts properties. 
-Transparency expert: Educates the public. Publishes the tax roll.

Assessment is a delicate hard job. Assessor's need the power of simulations to perform better.
